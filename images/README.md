# Adding Yakubu's Photo

## Quick Instructions

1. **Download a photo** of Yakubu Aiyegbeni from one of the sources below
2. **Save it** to this `images/` folder (e.g., `yakubu.jpg`)
3. **Update** `index.html` line 80-81 to point to your image:
   ```html
   <img src="images/yakubu.jpg"
        alt="Yakubu Aiyegbeni in action"
   ```

  - Historic 3-0 win photos (October 29, 2002)

  - Action shots in Maccabi Haifa green

### What to Look For

**Best images for this site:**
- ✅ Yakubu in Maccabi Haifa green jersey
- ✅ Action shots (shooting, celebrating)
- ✅ Portrait/headshot from 2001-2003 era
- ✅ High resolution (at least 800px wide)
- ✅ Good contrast and lighting

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Later career photos (Portsmouth, Everton, etc.)
- ❌ Low resolution/blurry images
- ❌ Heavily watermarked photos

## Image Specifications

**Recommended dimensions:**
- Width: 800-1200px
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 or 2:3 (portrait orientation works best)
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
