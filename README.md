# YAK ATTACK ⚽

A tribute website celebrating **Yakubu Aiyegbeni's** legendary career at **Maccabi Haifa FC** (2001-2003).

## 🌟 Live Site

**Visit:** [http://itayshmool.com/yakubu-haifa/](http://itayshmool.com/yakubu-haifa/)

## 🎨 Design

Retro sports magazine meets Israeli street culture - bold, playful, maximalist with nostalgic 2000s sports energy.

**Key Design Elements:**
- Maccabi Haifa's iconic green (#009739) and white colors
- VHS scanline effects and halftone patterns
- Diagonal energy cuts and overlapping composition
- Bold display typography mixing geometric forms
- Animated goal celebrations and retro effects

## 📊 Features

- **Mobile-first responsive design** - Perfect on all devices
- **Animated statistics** - Goals, games, and seasons counter
- **6 Legendary Matches** - Highlighting The Yak's greatest moments
- **Interactive elements** - Hover effects and Easter eggs
- **Accessibility** - Respects prefers-reduced-motion settings
- **Performance optimized** - Pure HTML/CSS/JS, no frameworks

## 🏆 The Legend

**31 goals in 66 appearances** across two electrifying seasons. Yakubu "The Yak" Aiyegbeni became an instant icon at Maccabi Haifa, dominating Israeli football with raw power and clinical finishing.

### Achievements at Maccabi Haifa
- 🏆 Israeli Premier League Champion (2001-02)
- 👑 Instant cult hero and fan favorite
- ⚽ Scored against Manchester United in the Champions League
- 🚀 Launched a legendary Premier League career

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Pure HTML5
- CSS3 (Custom properties, Grid, Flexbox, Animations)
- Vanilla JavaScript (Intersection Observer, smooth scroll)
- Google Fonts (Bebas Neue, Antonio, Barlow Condensed)
