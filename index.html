THE LEGEND

In 2001, a Nigerian striker arrived in Haifa and became an instant icon. Yakubu "The Yak" Aiyegbeni didn't just play football—he dominated Israeli football with raw power, clinical finishing, and an unstoppable hunger for goals.

⚡

31 goals in 66 appearances across two electrifying seasons (2001-2003). The Yak turned Kiryat Eliezer Stadium into his personal goal factory.

His performances earned him a dream move to Portsmouth FC, launching a legendary Premier League career. But Israeli fans never forgot the powerhouse striker who lit up the Israeli Premier League.
